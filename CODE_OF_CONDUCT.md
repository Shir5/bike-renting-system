# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming, harassment-free collaboration space for everyone, regardless of experience, identity, background, or ability.

Be respectful, assume good intent, give actionable technical feedback, and focus disagreement on ideas and evidence. Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, deliberate disruption, and publication of another person's private information are not acceptable.

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and restrict participation when behavior is harmful. Report conduct concerns privately through the maintainer profile linked from the repository. Reports will be reviewed confidentially and fairly.
